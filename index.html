Welcome to Guess Who?

Instructions

  1. Select a trial (1-4) to start a game. See Question History.

  2. Choose questions from the dropdown menu to narrow down possibilities.

  3. Click Confirm Question to see the answer and filter characters.

  4. When you think you know the answer, select a character and click Confirm Selection.

  5. Use Clear Selection to undo a character selection if selected (should be highlighted in blue).

  6. Exit & Save allows saving your game history in your download files.
